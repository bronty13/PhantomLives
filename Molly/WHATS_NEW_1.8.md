# What's new in Molly 💕 — v1.8

Hi Sallie 💖

Molly has some new little goodies for you in this update. None of it changes anything you already do day-to-day — your customers, clips, reminders, income, and journal are all exactly where you left them. These are *additions*, gently nestled in. Take a peek when you're ready.

---

## 🛍️ A whole new C4S Store tab

Look at your sidebar — there's a new **🛍️ C4S Store** entry sitting right under Clips. This is your read-only window into what's *actually* live on Clips4Sale for both stores. CoC on the left, PoA on the right, side by side whenever you want them.

Here's how it works:

1. In **C4S → Manage Clips**, scroll to the bottom and click **Export to CSV** (not Excel! — Molly will gently tell you if you pick the wrong one).
2. Save the file to your Downloads folder.
3. In Molly, click **🛍️ C4S Store → ✨ Import C4S CSV** and pick the file. Molly figures out whether it's a CoC or PoA export by herself, and shows you a little "Looks like a PoA export — sound right?" card so you can confirm.
4. Repeat for the other store.

That's it. Now you'll see:

- A **cute freshness banner** at the top — 🌸 fresh, ✨ still pretty fresh, 🌷 might be worth a re-import soon, 🌼 time for a fresh export. The font even shuffles every time you visit. ✨ Hide it from Settings if you'd rather not be reminded.
- **Total clips, lifetime sales, last-6-months income** as three big numbers up top.
- **Clips by status** bars — how many are `active`, `under_review`, etc.
- **Top 10 categories** and **Top 10 keywords** — what you lean on most. Sometimes surprising. 💕
- **Pricing** — min / mean / max across your live clips, at a glance.

### Drill-down ✨

This is the fun part. Click *anywhere* on the dashboard — a status row, a top category, a keyword chip — and Molly jumps you straight into the grid showing **just those clips**. The filter shows as a removable pill above the table (× to clear), and you can stack filters: status = `active` + category = `BBW`, then narrow further with the search box. Click **🗂 Grid** anytime to see everything again.

### The grid + detail page

When you do open the grid, you get a sortable table — click any column header to sort, click again to flip direction. Search bar with a regex checkbox. Click any row to open the **detail page**: full description in handwriting font, every C4S field laid out, with 📋 Copy buttons for title and ID. **← Back** keeps your search and sort intact.

### Pick your own columns

Settings → **🛍️ C4S** has a checkbox for every column. Persona and Title always show; everything else is yours to hide. Tracking Tag and Preview Filename default off because C4S leaves them empty anyway.

### If you ever want to start over

Same Settings page → **🗑 Delete all C4S data** (two-tap confirm). Wipes both stores' snapshots. Nothing else is touched. Re-import any time. 💕
