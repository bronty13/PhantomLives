## 🫧 Squish — never fight Slack about file size again!

You know that grumpy little message Slack gives you when a clip is too big?

> *"That file is too large and cannot be uploaded. The limit is 1 GB."* 😤

Ugh. Your gorgeous **4K** videos can be **4 GB or even bigger**, and Slack just
won't take them. So I made you a fix, and it's *the* easiest thing. 💕

There's a new **🫧 Squish** tab in my sidebar. Here's the whole dance:

1. Tap **📁 Pick a video** and choose your big chonky clip.
2. I'll peek at it and tell you how big it is — *"ooh, that's a chonky 4.2 GB 🫢"*.
3. Tap **🫧 Squish it under 1 GB!** — and that's it! Go sip your coffee ☕.

I shrink it down to a lovely **Full-HD** size and squeeze out all the extra
megabytes, keeping it **as crisp and pretty as I possibly can** while making sure
it lands safely **under 1 GB** so Slack will happily take it. 🩷

When I'm done I'll show you the happy news — *"Squished! 4.2 GB → 0.9 GB 🫧"* —
and your new slimmer clip is waiting in your **Downloads → Molly** folder, named
like `YourClip (Squished).mov`. Tap **📂 Show in folder** to pop right to it, then
drag it into Slack. 🎀

---

## 🌸 A few little reassurances

- **Your original is never, ever touched.** I make a fresh squished *copy* and
  leave your big beautiful original exactly where it was. 💕
- **Big videos take a few minutes** — a long 4K clip has a *lot* of pixels to
  tuck in! My progress bar keeps you company the whole time. 🫧
- **Already under 1 GB?** I'll let you know — but you can still squish it
  smaller if you'd like a teenier file.
- It all happens **right on your computer** — nothing gets uploaded anywhere. 🔒
