# What's new in Molly 💕 — v1.32

Hi Sallie 💖

This one's a little money-saver for your brain: your **Content bundles** can now
have a **price**, and I'll *figure out a good one for you* based on how long your
videos are. No more squinting at a chart — just drop your clips in and I'll do
the math. 🪙✨

---

## 1. 💲 I price your Content bundles for you

You know how **Custom bundles** already have a price? Now **Content bundles** do
too — and the best part is **I default it for you**.

As you add videos to a Content bundle, I quietly add up how long they all are,
and right under your files you'll see a little card: **Total video length** and
my **Suggested price**. The longer the set, the higher I price it — and I always
land on a pretty `$X.99`. 🌟

A few of my go-to numbers (you can change the formula — see below):

- about **4 minutes** → **$8.99**
- about **6 minutes** → **$10.99**
- about **8 minutes** → **$12.99**
- **12 minutes+** → **$16.99**

> *Try it for me:* make a **＋ New Content Bundle**, drop in a couple of real
> videos, and peek at the little **Price** card under your files — does the
> total length look right, and a sweet suggested price next to it? 🎀

---

## 2. ✅ You confirm (or change) the price on the review page

When you hit **🎁 Review & Publish…**, you'll find a **Price** spot all ready to
go — I've already filled in my suggestion. From there it's totally yours:

- Happy with it? Leave it. 💕
- Want a different number? Just type it.
- Giving this one away? Tick **Free** and the box tucks itself away.
- Changed your clips and want my fresh number back? Tap **↺ Reset to suggested**
  and I'll recompute from your current videos — even un-tick **Free** for you.

I only fill in my suggestion *once*, so a price you've chosen yourself is always
safe — I'll never sneak in and overwrite it. 🥰

> *Try it for me:* on the review page, tick **Free** then un-tick it (the price
> comes right back), and give **↺ Reset to suggested** a tap. Did the box behave
> sweetly each time?

---

## 3. 🎛️ Want different prices? Tune me in Settings

Pop into **Settings → Bundler → Content bundle pricing** and you'll find three
little dials — a **base price**, a **per-minute** rate, and a **floor** (my
lowest price). There's a live preview so you can see exactly what a 4-, 6-, 8-,
and 12-minute bundle would cost as you nudge them. The pretty `$X.99` ending is
always on. 🧁
