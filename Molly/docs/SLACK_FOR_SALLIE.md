**1. Download Molly**

Open this link in your browser:

(If your browser warns you about the file — pop-up like "this file might be dangerous" — click **Keep**. It's just Windows being shy about apps it hasn't seen before.)

---

**2. Run the installer**

Open your **Downloads** folder (Windows + E, then click Downloads on the left).

Double-click **Molly_1.0.0_x64-setup.exe**.

🚨 **Windows might pop up a blue screen that says "Windows protected your PC".** This is normal and not scary — it's because Molly is a brand-new app the Microsoft cloud hasn't catalogued yet. Click **More info** (small link), then click **Run anyway**.

The installer opens. Just click through — every default option is fine. It takes about 10 seconds. When it says "Finish", click Finish.

---

**3. Open Molly**

Look on your **Desktop** — there's a new icon called **Molly** with a pink M and a heart. Double-click it.

(If you don't see it on the desktop: hit the **Windows key** and just type "Molly" and hit enter.)

The first time she opens she'll take a couple seconds to set up. When the window appears you'll see:
- A pink Molly logo on the left
- A persona switcher at the top right: **CoC** / **PoA** / **Sa** / **★ All**

Click each persona pill to see the whole app recolor. That's her working. The colors are yours and they're configurable in Settings if you ever want to tweak them.

**4. Things to try first**

In rough order of "you'll want to know about this":

**If anything weird happens**

You really can't break her. The auto-backup in `Downloads\Molly backup\` is your safety net.
