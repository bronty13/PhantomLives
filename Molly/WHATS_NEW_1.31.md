# What's new in Molly 💕 — v1.31

Hi Sallie 💖

Your **▶️ YouTube bundles** got a little glow-up this time — three sweet new
touches, all living right in the YouTube bundle form. Nothing else changed, and
nothing you've already made is affected. Come see! 🦋

---

## 1. 🖼️ Every YouTube video gets a thumbnail now

YouTube shows a cover picture for every video — so I made sure your bundles
carry one. Open a **▶️ YouTube Bundle** and right under the description there's
a brand-new **🖼️ Thumbnail Image** spot. Drop in a JPG or PNG (up to 5 MB)…

…**or** — and this is my favorite part — tap **✨ Grab a frame from a video** and
snip the *perfect* still straight off one of your clips, without ever leaving
me. You'll see a little preview pop in the second it's saved. 🌟

It's a **required** little darling now (just like the description), so I'll
gently keep the **Approve & Publish** button resting until it's in — no
half-dressed videos going out the door. 💅

> *Try it for me:* open a YouTube bundle, tap **✨ Grab a frame from a video**,
> and pick a cute moment. Did the preview show up right away?

---

## 2. 🔒 "Make private" — for videos that go live the moment you publish

Right where the go-live date used to sit, there's now a **🔒 Make private**
checkbox, and it starts **checked**. When it's on, your video goes up *private*
and goes live the instant you publish — so there's nothing to schedule, and I
tuck the date picker away for you. Easy. ✨

Want to schedule it instead? Just **uncheck** it — the **Go-live date** slides
right back, with my usual *"are you allowing enough time for editing?"* nudge if
it's coming up fast. 🌷

You can pick your *favorite* starting state in **Settings → Bundler → YouTube
bundle defaults** — if you almost always go private, leave it on; if you almost
always schedule, flip the default off. Your call, always. 💕

> *Try it for me:* make a YouTube bundle, leave **🔒 Make private** checked, and
> notice the date picker is gone. Then uncheck it and watch the date field come
> back. 🪄

---

## 3. 🎀 "Also Post SFW ManyVids" — a little Yes/No for Robert

At the very bottom of the YouTube form there's a tiny **Also Post SFW ManyVids**
toggle — **No** by default. Tap **Yes** any time you'd also like Robert to post
a SFW cut to ManyVids, and your choice rides along in the bundle's notes so he
sees it. (Default's configurable in that same Settings card, naturally.) 🥰

> *Try it for me:* flip it to **Yes** on a bundle, then open **▶️ Review &
> Publish** and check that **Also post SFW ManyVids: Yes** shows up in the
> review. 🎁
