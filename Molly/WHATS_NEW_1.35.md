# What's new in Molly 💕 — v1.35

Hi Sallie 💖

This one's all about keeping your videos **safe** and your bundles **easy to
send**, even the really big ones. A few little guardian angels, all working
quietly in the background for you. 🌷

---

## 1. 📦 Big bundles now split themselves for Slack

You know how Slack won't let you upload anything bigger than **1 GB**? Some of
your bundles (especially chunky video ones) sneak past that even after
squishing. So now, if a bundle comes out too big, **I automatically cut it into
tidy little pieces** — each one small enough for Slack. 💕

When that happens, the finish screen shows you the pieces and says exactly what
to do: **send Robert *all* of them** (any order is fine!). His SideMolly stitches
them right back together on his end and checks everything lined up perfectly — you
don't have to do a single extra thing. And small bundles? Totally unchanged, still
one neat file. 🎁

> *Try it for me:* next time you publish a big one, peek at the finish screen —
> did I show you a little list of pieces to send? 🎀

---

## 2. 🚨 A gentle nudge when your disk is getting full

Your Mac gets *cozy* on space sometimes, and low disk space is exactly what can
make a video come out broken. So now, on the **🎁 Bundles** and **🗜️ Squish**
pages, I'll show you a soft heads-up:

- 🌷 **under 3 GB free** — a friendly "maybe tidy up soon" note
- 🚨 **under 1 GB free** — a firmer "let's free some space first" note

There's a **🔄 Recheck** button right on it — clear out some files, tap Recheck,
and if you've made room the message just melts away. 💖 If things are *really*
tight, I'll politely wait to squish or bundle until there's room, so we never
make a broken file by accident.

---

## 3. 🛡️ I won't let a broken video slip through anymore

This is the big, quiet one. Before, if a squish got interrupted (usually from
low disk), the video could come out **damaged but still look finished** — and it
could sneak into a bundle. Not anymore:

- **Squish now double-checks its work** — it makes sure the shrunk video actually
  opens and plays start-to-finish *before* saving it. If something went wrong,
  it tells you (usually "free up some disk space and try again") instead of
  handing you a broken file. 💪
- **Publishing double-checks too** — when you hit publish, I quietly test every
  video first. If one won't play, I stop and point right at it with a red ⛔ so
  you can re-add or re-squish it — *before* it ever reaches Robert. 🌸

And for the curious/techy bits: every bundle now tucks a tiny **diagnostics
note** inside it, so if anything ever acts up, Robert has the details to figure
it out fast. You never have to look at it — it's just there if we need it. 📋
