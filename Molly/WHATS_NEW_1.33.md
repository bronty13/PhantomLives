# What's new in Molly 💕 — v1.33

Hi Sallie 💖

Little keepsake update! When Robert sends back a bundle from **SideMolly**, it
now carries a tidy **summary report** — a one-page recap of the whole bundle —
and from now on I'll **catch it and keep it for you**, right on the bundle. 📄✨

---

## 1. 📄 Your bundle's summary report, saved inside me

You know how Robert posts a bundle and sends you back a **return file**? Newer
return files have a little **summary PDF** tucked inside. When you import one,
I lift that PDF out and store it safely **inside Molly** — so you never need to
keep the original ZIP around just to read the recap. 🥰

It's the whole bundle at a glance: what it was, what went where, all in one
pretty page Robert put together for you.

---

## 2. 👀 Find it right on the bundle

Open any bundle from the **🎁 Bundles** page and look at the very top. If a
summary came home with that bundle, you'll see a soft **📄 Summary report**
card waiting there, with two little buttons:

- **Open report** — pops it open in your usual PDF viewer. 💕
- **⬇ Download** — saves your own copy anywhere you like (I'll suggest a name).

If a bundle *doesn't* show the card, that just means its return file didn't
carry a summary — older ones won't, and that's totally okay. The card only
appears when there's really something to open, so it'll never clutter up a
bundle that doesn't have one. 🌸

> *Try it for me:* import a fresh return file from Robert, then open that bundle
> and tap **Open report** at the top. Did your recap pop right open? 🎀
