# Schema Library Proposals

A brainstorm of schemas **not yet in the library** that we could add next. The current library ships **251 templates** across 13 categories. This document lists ~300+ additional candidates for the next round, organized for community voting.

**Status:** proposals only, none of these are implemented. The pattern is established: each accepted idea becomes one `Entry` in `Models/SchemaLibrary+ExtendedCatalog.swift` with a category, blurb, search keywords, and an `ObjectType` template wired up to view defaults.

**How to vote:** mark your top picks (✅), nopes (❌), or "would use this" (👍) next to any entry. We'll prioritize the highest-signal candidates for the next batch.

## Productivity & Planning

- **Tickler file entry** — Defer something until a specific date and resurface it ("revisit this in 6 weeks").
- **Atomic next-action** — GTD-style single physical next action, separate from project.
- **Stop-doing list** — Things you're consciously NOT going to do anymore.
- **Personal manifesto** — Operating principles, values, working agreements you live by.
- **Daily highlight** — A single must-do for the day (Make Time methodology).
- **Top three** — Three priorities for the day. Lighter than a full planner.
- **After-action review** — Military-style retro on a discrete event: plan vs reality vs lessons.
- **Pre-mortem** — Imagine the project failed; what would the post-mortem say?
- **1-3-5 list** — One big thing, three medium, five small. Daily completion log.
- **Eisenhower matrix item** — Urgent/important quadrant tagging.
- **Tomorrow setup** — Pre-bed prep: what's on tap, what's laid out, what's blocked.
- **Operating principles** — Personal or team — short rules you've committed to.
- **Personal user manual** — "How to work with me" doc, version-tracked.
- **Capabilities inventory** — Things you can confidently do well, with evidence.
- **Time saver / hack** — A specific trick you found; tag by domain.
- **Meeting prep** — Notes ahead of a meeting (separate from the meeting log itself).
- **Outsourced item** — Delegated tasks: who, what, status, deadline.
- **Annual planning** — Yearly themes, big rocks, hopes.
- **Five-year vision** — Long-horizon picture; revisited annually.
- **Implementation intention** — "If X happens, I will do Y." Habit science.
- **Habit stack** — Linked chains of habits (after coffee → meditate → journal).
- **Energy block** — Calendar-style "deep work between 9–11" scheduled blocks.
- **Time audit week entry** — Where did the hour actually go? For week-long audits.
- **Daily intention** — "Today I will…" written each morning.
- **Lessons applied** — When you actually used a past lesson; closes the loop.
