# Further reading — books, guides, blogs, research, communities

Populated as lessons land. Seed canon:

- **Apple** — Platform Security Guide; Platform Deployment Guide; developer.apple.com; Legal
  Process Guidelines (US).
- **Internals** — Jonathan Levin, *MacOS and iOS Internals* vols I–III (newosxbook.com);
  theapplewiki.com; Project Zero; arXiv 2510.09272 (SPTM/TXM/Exclaves).
- **Forensics** — Sarah Edwards (mac4n6.com, APOLLO); Alexis Brignoni (iLEAPP); SANS
  FOR585/FOR518; d204n6 / Ian Whiffin (Biome/SEGB); Yogesh Khatri (mac_apt); Josh Hickman
  (thebinaryhick.blog) + Digital Corpora sample images; vendor research blogs.
- **Dev/RE** — OWASP MAS (MASVS/MASTG); Frida & objection docs; *iOS Application Security*
  (David Thiel); *The Mobile Application Hacker's Handbook*.
- **Wireless** — owlink.org / SEEMOO (AWDL, PrivateDrop, Find My); OpenHaystack.
