How to Play 🍳

🎯 The goal

Orders arrive as little tickets. Cook the dish, plate it, and pop it through the serving window before the customer's patience bar runs out. Whoever banks more points before the clock hits zero — you or Chef Byte — wins the round.

🕹️ Controls

WASD / arrow keys move. Space or E picks up, puts down, pours and serves — it always does the sensible thing for whatever you're facing. Prefer the mouse? Click any station and your chef walks over and uses it. Click the floor to stroll.

🔪 Prep

Grab ingredients from crates. Pop choppables on a cutting board and stand at it — your chef chops away automatically. Chopped tomatoes, onions and patties go in a stove pot. Soup needs three of the same veg!

🍽️ Plate & serve

Take a plate from the stack, hold it against prepared food to scoop it on (a done pot pours straight onto your plate), then bring it to the serving window. Buns hop onto a held plate right from their crate.

🔥 Don't burn it

A finished pot keeps sizzling — leave it too long and it burns. Scrape a burnt pot with empty hands, take a deep breath, start again. The bin forgives all mistakes.

💰 Scoring

Each dish pays its base price + a tip that shrinks as the customer waits. Serve tickets in order to build a tip multiplier up to 4× — miss an order and the combo (and a few points) evaporate. Score enough for ⭐, ⭐⭐ or ⭐⭐⭐, and win matches to fill your Trophy Shelf!
